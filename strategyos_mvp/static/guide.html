What StrategyOS does

StrategyOS brings strategy, performance, cases, and supporting evidence into one review surface so leaders can see what matters and where the proof sits.

It is designed for teams that currently rely on scattered spreadsheets, disconnected reports, and decision notes that are hard to trace back to source material.

ExecutiveSees the top-level picture: KPIs, cases, and a board-pack snapshot.
Business-unit leaderReviews results and risks for their area.
ReviewerInspects the evidence behind numbers and findings.
OperatorPrepares data and runs the analysis workflow.

How it works

  1. 1Prepare. Source material such as spreadsheets, financial extracts, plans, and review notes is loaded into a controlled workspace.
  2. 2Analyse. The system produces findings, flags gaps, and connects each case to supporting evidence.
  3. 3Review. A human reviewer inspects, approves, or challenges the output before it reaches leadership.

Human review stays central

The system prepares the view and connects evidence. A person still makes the final call, especially before a finding is shared with leadership or used in a board pack.

The full governed workflow requires an authenticated session. The temporary /login page lets invited testers enter as different roles without exposing private production credentials.

What you can see right now

  • /Homepage. An executive overview with KPI cards, case previews, an evidence packet preview, and a board-pack area.
  • /executiveExecutive overview. The same leadership view, framed for review of current performance signals.
  • /loginCustomer sign-in. Choose your authorized account and enter its password to continue.
  • /appWorkspace preview. The governed diagnostics workspace layout after a temporary login.

The public surface is a preview. Live case data, full evidence, reports, and approval actions require an authorised session; the temporary test users are only for preview and role-flow testing.

Try it yourself

Estimated time: 5–10 minutes. Use one of the temporary test users on /login; the password list is shared separately with authorised testers.

  1. 1Open https://strategyos.live/login.
  2. 2Select a temporary role user, enter its password, and press Log in.
  3. 3Executive users land on /executive; all other test roles land on /app.
  4. 4Check the role-specific workspace: KPI cards, case list, evidence packet area, board-pack area, and allowed actions.
  5. 5Repeat with another role to compare what an operator, reviewer, auditor, executive, tenant admin, or system user can see and do.
  6. 6Use only the temporary test users for preview testing; real customer credentials and production data are out of scope for this page.
